🌌 Our Solar System

Explore the planets that revolve around our star — the Sun!

☀️ Sun

The Sun is the center of our solar system, providing light and heat to all the planets.

🌍 Earth

Our home planet, known for its life-supporting atmosphere and blue oceans.

🪐 Saturn

Famous for its spectacular ring system made of ice and rock particles.

🔥 Mercury

The closest planet to the Sun and the smallest in our solar system.

🌀 Jupiter

The largest planet, known for its Great Red Spot and powerful storms.

🔴 Mars

Called the "Red Planet" because of its reddish surface caused by iron oxide.

🟣 Venus

The hottest planet due to its thick, toxic atmosphere full of greenhouse gases.

🔵 Neptune

A cold, blue planet with strong winds, located farthest from the Sun.

🧊 Uranus

Tilted on its side, this ice giant has faint rings and a pale blue color.
